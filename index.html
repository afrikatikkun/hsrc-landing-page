Empowering South Africa Through Digital Skills

The Human Sciences Research Council (HSRC) has partnered with Mzansi Digital Learning to bring curated digital skills development to South Africans. Our goal is to bridge the digital divide by providing access to high-quality, industry-relevant training that empowers individuals to thrive in the modern economy.

Note: Courses on this portal are professionally recognised. They are not formally accredited by a SETA or QCTO, but are valued by employers and aligned with industry standards.
